The traditional colors of Mardi Gras are purple, green, and gold.The Three shades are said to symbolize justice, faith, and power, respectively.

By law, float riders must always have a mask on. On Fat Tuesday, masking is legal for everyone else, and the elaborate masks that some wear add to the fun.

The tradition of float riders throwing trinkets to the crowds began in the 1870s, and continues today. Typical throws include beads, cups, doubloons and stuffed animals. for more...
