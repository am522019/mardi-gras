Sheraton New Orleans Hotel

Jim Cook

Jim Cook is the General Manager at the Sheraton New Orleans Hotel. Employed by Marriott International, formerly Starwood Hotels & Resorts Worldwide since 1999 where he was the Director of Food and Beverage at the Sheraton Norfolk Waterside Hotel. Since then Jim has held General Manager roles at former Starwood Hotels in Virginia Beach, VA. Allentown PA, and Hyannis, MA. He has also held a positon as Hotel Manager at the Sheraton Boston where he repositioned the hotel, returning it to its former status as one of the iconic hotels in the Sheraton Hotel Portfolio. Jim also worked in the Franchise Division as Senior Director of Operations for the Northeast region supporting a portfolio 39 hotels across New England, New Jersey, and New York. Jim and his two daughters moved to New Orleans in June 2012 and he now serves as General Manager of the Sheraton New Orleans Hotel.
